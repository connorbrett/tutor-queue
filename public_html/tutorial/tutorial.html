Project overview

At the tutor center, the students can ask for help with their assignments/projects from the tutors. Conventionally, the student queue is managed by hundreds of Google Sheets rows which makes it slow and unorganized. Thus, this website is created to make the process faster, organized, and more convinient.

Student

From the homepage, click on the student symbol to access the student site. You should now see a form with various fields including name, email address, and other essentials information to submit your request. When clicked on "send" button, your request will be sent and there will be a notification to indicate that. You can also submit another request once your previous request has been sent succesfully. The average waiting time is 7 minutes or more depends on the number of waiting students in the queue so please be patient while you are waiting, thank you! *Note: the student site will show alert accordingly if required informations are blank or the student is already in the queue.


Tutor

From the homepage, click on the tutor symbol to access the tutor site. A login window will popup, and you can use your tutor account to login. Please refer to Project overview for a dummy account. Once logged in, you can see 2 queues includes "waiting" and "in-progress". The "waiting" queue contains the current awaiting students, and the "in-progress" queue contains your student you are assigned to right now. If a tutor is not busy (blank in-progress queue), then he/she can help the next student by clicking on the "waiting" button under the status column. Otherwise, the tutor can finish the student request by clicking on the "done" button.

The tutors will be notified every 10 seconds if there are students currently in the waiting-queue. In that case, the tab will change as below.

Coordinator

From the homepage, click on the UofA logo to access the coordinator site. You can now see the login page for tutor coordinator, and there is also a creating account feature which is for testing purpose.
Once logged in, you can see the tutor website as below.
This website helps the coordinators to manage the tutors (e.g: shift time, tutor account). The coordinators can create a new tutor account with information such as name, email, courses. They can also see the details of the tutor's shifts and related informations which are inside an embeded Google Sheet.
